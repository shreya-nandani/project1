🌿 Home Remedies & Yoga Guide

Click on a condition card to see remedies & yoga.

🤕 Headache ▼

Remedies: Drink ginger tea, apply peppermint oil on temples, stay hydrated.

Yoga: Child’s Pose, Forward Bend, Cat-Cow.

🩸 Period Pain ▼

Remedies: Warm compress, ginger tea, avoid junk food.

Yoga: Reclined Bound Angle Pose, Supine Twist.

🤒 Fever ▼

Remedies: Tulsi & ginger tea, sponge bath, hydration.

Yoga: Anulom-Vilom, Supported Bridge.

🚗🤢 Car Sickness ▼

Remedies: Chew ginger, fresh air, avoid reading.

Yoga: Deep breathing, Sukhasana.

🌬️ Breathing Issues ▼

Remedies: Steam inhalation, warm water, avoid cold air.

Yoga: Anulom-Vilom, Bhramari, Bhujangasana.

🩹 Wound Healing ▼

Remedies: Turmeric paste, aloe vera, keep clean.

Yoga: Gentle restorative yoga.

✂️ Cuts ▼

Remedies: Wash, apply honey or aloe vera, bandage.

Yoga: Focus on meditation & breathwork.

🩺 Diabetes ▼

Remedies: Karela juice, avoid sugar, eat fiber-rich foods.

Yoga: Kapalbhati, Ardha Matsyendrasana.

🤰 Morning Sickness ▼

Remedies: Small meals, ginger tea, avoid strong smells.

Yoga: Prenatal yoga, breathing, Shavasana.

🐍 Snake Bite ▼

⚠️ Emergency: Needs immediate medical care. Keep still, tie above bite, rush to hospital.

Yoga/Remedies: None. Medical help only.

💪 Body Pain ▼

Remedies: Warm bath, massage with oil, turmeric milk.

Yoga: Surya Namaskar, Child’s Pose.

🌸 Acne & Pimples ▼

Remedies: Aloe vera gel, neem paste, drink water, avoid oily food.

Yoga: Kapalbhati, Sarvangasana.
